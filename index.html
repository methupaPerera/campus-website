About Us...

Our Vision

To produce the highest number of Sri Lankan intelligent people with local and foreign knowledge.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide job-oriented practical knowledge through all courses

This is an institution that has been providing the highest (A) pass and university entrance in Information and Communication Technology to O/L and A/L students for more than 5 years starting from the year 2016. At present, it is also a registered institute for Diploma and Certificate Courses.

Our main objective is to develop international level practical knowledge for local employment needs.This institute will produce not only an educated citizen but also an intelligent and capable citizen who has the practical knowledge suitable for the job and will be able to cater to the needs of the job entrepreneurs. Experienced and knowledgeable lecturers work here. Every student who joins us and completes the course will have the opportunity to work boldly.
